About

This website is about dynamical systems. Its main goal is to share python programs for numerical simulations and creating figures, and to propose an interface for simulating dynamical systems.

It is powered by Django, and hosted at PythonAnywhere.

Contact

I am a mathematics teacher in Le Havre, Normandy, France. If you want to contact me, just send an email.
